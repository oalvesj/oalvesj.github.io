About

Versatility

I'm a tech professional with a strong analytical background — and my career path reflects that combination. Since January 2026, I've been working as a Full Stack Web Developer at a European health tech company, building and maintaining software used by medical clinics. My stack is centered on PHP, SQL Server, and JavaScript. Beyond writing code, my role involves understanding clinical workflows, translating business requirements into technical solutions — from patient records to tablet-based digital signature processes — and integrating with legacy desktop systems. I also incorporate AI-assisted development into my engineering routine to accelerate delivery.

I'm currently completing two Technological Specialization Courses (CETs) in Portugal — IT Programming and Multimedia.

I pivoted into tech in 2021, when I started a degree in Systems Analysis in Brazil. After 12 months, I put it on hold due to an international, for educational purposes.

What sets me apart from most developers is nearly two decades of experience in business communications, economics, and corporate analysis. That background gives me the ability to understand business logic, communicate clearly with stakeholders, and document processes with precision — skills that are hard to teach and impossible to shortcut.

I'm especially interested in roles where business meets technology — Systems Analysis, Functional Analysis, or Business Analysis — where that combined experience becomes a real asset.
